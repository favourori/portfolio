BETHEL CHURCH, MAGNOLIA

Developing tailored Destop & Mobile (Andriod & iOS) solution for seamless church management.

THE PROBLEM :

I came to Bethel church in the Fall of 2016, and since then, we've been printing our weekly bulletins, news & events on papers. Over the Years, I believe they've spent thousands of Dollars on cost of Printing. Worst was, after spending money on all these printings, not everybody cared to read them.

I decided to replace this 'system' with something more seamless. Something that's going to cut all costs, and will be easily accessed from all locations, so I developed the Bethel Church's Mobile App.


THE SOLUTION :

  • Develop A Java desktop App which serves as an interface where contents (Video, Audio, Newsletters, Bulletins & Sermons) will be uploaded to a DB (MySQL)
  • Design the Android App with Android Studio (Java) & the iOS app with Xcode (Swift) which will fetch all contents from the DB
